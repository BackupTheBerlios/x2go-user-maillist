> My Problem: i do connect to several servers via vpn but only one at a
> time. Inside the vpn the terminalservers name is allways kmux-svc and
> his ip is allways the same.

> If i try to connect to the second kmux-svc i'll get the message that
> the host key did change and i have to remove it from the key-file than
> the new one is entered.
>
> Could you please give me an idea how to obtain such a setup without
> manually editing the ssh stuff?
>

On the remote KMUX-svc you could use a tool like autossh to create a  
tunnel from

   kmux-svc:<custom-port> to kmux-svc:22

With you X2Go Client (or PyHoca-GUI, have you tried that, as it will  
fit your needs better, I guess) you then connect to  
kmux-svc:<custom-port>. Note, for every such server you have to use a  
different <custom-port>.

BTW: you should experience the same issue with normal SSH(!?!?).
