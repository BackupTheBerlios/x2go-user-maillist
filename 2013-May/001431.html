> get a connection and the volume fader is greyed out. The sound cannot be
> restored by de-selecting sound for that session.

Client-side is Linux or Windows?

With Linux, there normally is a pulseaudio daemon running locally. You  
have to make sure to enable network socket binding for this local  
daemon.

If client-side is Windows, the pulseaudio daemon is fully handled by  
X2Go Client.

The server-side pulseaudio daemon is irrelevant for X2Go sessions and  
should not even be started (disable in autostart settings of your  
server-side desktop shell).

Does that help already?
