Yes, the x2goldaptools are not in our repositories anymore. The  
current plan is to document what attributes have to available in LDAP  
to make an X2go server an X2go server.

My personal approach is providing you the means of tweaking LDAP  
servers already existing at sites. However, this is all work in  
progress.

As a start, you could set up an LDAP server as described here (on the  
referenced pages):
http://wiki.debian.org/LDAP

The tweaks needed for X2go will follow in the next 2-4 weeks.
