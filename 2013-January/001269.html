I am going to comprehend how this gentle thing works!
I solved the problem. In the /etc/cups/cups-x2go.conf File I commented
out the line

#ps2pdf = /usr/bin/gs -q -dCompatibilityLevel=1.4 -dNOPAUSE -dBATCH
-dSAFER -sDEVICE=pdfwrite -sOutputFile=\"$

Now printing works fine from WindowsClient!
