> Hi there,

> Around 2 weeks ago we came across the release of 4.x and me and other
> user (sorry, can't remember his name) talk about problems on the Ubuntu
> desktop resolution matches the same one as the client.
>
> Was that considered a bug and is there an ETA for a fix?

No, it is not considered a bug. You have to edit /etc/x2go/x2goagent.options.

For Kubuntu: disable the XFIXES extension (now the default).

For Unity: leave the XFIXES extension active (comment out the  
corresponding line in x2goagent.options).
