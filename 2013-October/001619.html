Hi Mathias,

On  Di 01 Okt 2013 14:03:56 CEST, Mathias Ewald wrote:

> since I cannot get x2gobroker to work  yet - see my other mail - I  
> have to ask: doe x2gobroker tunnel the connection from client to  
> server or just simply tell the client where to connect to? Or  
> putting it differently: Does my client need routed IP connectivity  
> to the server? I am looking for a way to save public IP addresses.

No, at the time being the session broker does not offer tunneling by  
itself. However, this can be easily achieved by the SSH proxy feature  
available in X2Go Client.

Simply provide session profiles via X2Go Session Broker that use the  
session broker itself as proxy. Of course, this means that your users  
will be able to SSH to the session broker itself. If you do not want  
that, a more intrinsic solution can be contracted from me (i.e. paid  
development work).
