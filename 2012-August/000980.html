The cause of my problem was a configuration setting pointing to a
"user defined X-server" which did not exist.

After changing to "integrated X-server" everything worked smoothly.

> X-Server lässt sich nicht starten. Bitte überprüfen Sie Ihre
> Installation.
> (X-server cannot be started, please, check your installation.)

It would be helpful to change the popup to something like
"X-server cannot be started, please, check your X-server configuration in the X2Go client."
