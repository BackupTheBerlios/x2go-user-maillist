Hi Jochen,

On Di 18 Dez 2012 18:47:33 CET Jochen Schulz wrote:

> We are extensively using X2go, and are very happy, that this solution
> exist. A we have several computers which can hold x2go sessions, it
> becomes more vital to administer these sessions from a central point.

Sure!

> Of course there are x2golistsessions, x2goterminate-session and so on
> but would it be nice to have a simple GUI or at least a little bit more
> readable terminal-output?

There is a GUI in prep called x2goadmincenter. See:  
x2goadmincenter.git on http://git.x2go.org

However, the development of this GUI is currently stalling as there  
are other projects that are contracted projects in the queue first  
(contracted == payed-for).

Note: by contracting one of the developers, you can speed up solutions  
for your setup.

> Also: The x2goclient doesn't seem to have a gui-part to administer
> suspended and running sessions. This for example would made it much
> easier to handle your running sessions. Especially, if you are using
> several different servers (This is all without the
> thinclient-environment in mind!)

A session (profile) management for X2Go Session is also in  
preparation, see x2gobroker.git on http://git.x2go.org

If you need further info on both projects and their status, contact us  
(mainly me and Alex) during daytime (CEST) on IRC: #x2go  
irc.freenode.org.
