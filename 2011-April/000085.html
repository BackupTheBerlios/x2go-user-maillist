desktop sharing has been implemented and I use it for customer care sometimes.

You can share a same user's desktop from two client sessions in rw  
mode. You can get read-only access to another user's desktop. However,  
this limitation is coded into one of the x2go scripts. Thus, I guess,  
that it should be possible to get rw-desktop sharing across account  
borders.
