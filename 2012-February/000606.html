On Thursday 16 February 2012 15:17:59 Paul van der Vlis wrote:
> Are here people with experiences with laptops for thin-client use?
> I need 7 laptops for use with X2go.

Using the same atom-based systems in desktop-thinclients as they would be in 
netbooks, we have very good experience. Using netbooks/notebooks for 
thinclients with only the wifi will be slow and sluggish and will not allow 
network-boot...

And when you confine a network-booting laptop to desktop-only usage with a 
cable, its cheaper and more ergonomic to buy a big screen, a real keyboard and 
mice and just plug the small thinclient to the back of the screen.

Shameless plug: http://www.b-c-s.de, we sell such small machines for exactly 
that purpose... (also smaller ones than depicted in the flyer.)

Have fun,
