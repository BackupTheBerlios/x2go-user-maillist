Hi all,

I have added a much nicer boot splash to the X2Go Thin Client Environment.

I also added background support for X2Go Client withint the X2Go TCE.  
(X2Go Client has had the --background option for a long time already,  
now it is being used within the TCE by default).

You have to build your thinclient chroot from scratch  
(x2gothinclient_create) and run x2gothinclient_preptftpd again. Backup  
/srv/tftp and /opt/x2gothinclient before doing this.

Happy testing,
Mike
