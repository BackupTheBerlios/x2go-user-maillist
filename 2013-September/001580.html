> Google two factor authentication Linux PAM module and Google  
> Authenticator App for iOS or Android.

What a coincidence... I am working on something very similar at the moment.

In OpenSSH 6.2 there will be a sshd_config parameter  
AuthenticationMethods. With this parameter OpenSSH will require  
two-factor auth (key+password e.g.).

I am currently implementing this in Python X2Go / PyHoca-GUI.
