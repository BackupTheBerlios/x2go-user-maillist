On Do 20 Sep 2012 08:23:52 CEST Lukas Laukamp wrote:

> Hello all,
>
> I have a question about PyHoca. When I have two PCs for example one  
> at home and one at work and start a session with PyHoca on the PC at  
> home, why can't I resume this session with the PyHoca on the PC at  
> work. Is there a way that makes it possible to resume this session  
> at the PC at work or something like this? So I always have to start  
> a second session and thats not the best because so I can't use  
> already running programs with the state they have in the first  
> session.
>
> Hope that someone can help me.

That should basically be possible.

There is only one limitation that I hard-coded into Python X2Go (the  
X2Go backend in PyHoca): if the color depth differs between clients,  
you cannot resume sessions from one client to another.

So, please check the colour depth on your client systems. If they are  
equal, please report back, and we have to investigate further.
