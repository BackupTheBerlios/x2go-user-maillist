Hello,

the RPMs which are located in the OpenSUSE Buildservices 
(https://build.opensuse.org/project/show?project=X11%3ARemoteDesktop%3Ax2go) 
are working fine. I tested them a few weeks ago on a CentOS 6 (should 
also work on 5.x line). The only thing you have to make sure is that the 
x2goserver runs in runlevel 5 because he accepts connections only in 
this runlevel.
