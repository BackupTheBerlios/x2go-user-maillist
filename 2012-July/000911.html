Hallo X2Go users,

We are running X2Go in a TCE environment on debian squeeze. Three types of 
thin clients are in use:

1) Shuttle X27D, Atom 330, Intel 945GC ICH7 chipset, Intel GMA950
2) Shuttle XS35V2, Atom 525, Intel NM10 Express chipset, Intel GMA3150
3) Shuttle XS36V, Atom D2700, Intel NM10 Express chipset Intel GMA3650

With the standard kernel 2.6.32-686, we didn't manage to run higher 
resolutions than 1280x1024 on all of them, neither on the greeting screen nor 
on the Gnome desktop (Ubuntu 10.04). I embedded kernel 3.2.0 in the boot 
environment. With it, the Shuttle X27D works fine with a resolution up to 
1920x1080, but the preferred fanless XS35V2 and XS36V boxes still do not work 
with higher resolutions than 1280x1024.

You will find further informations in the attachment, The two graphics GMA950 
and GMA3650 are considered. Log's from GMA3150 look similar to the GMA3650 
ones. The error message "(EE) open /dev/fb0: No such file or directory" in the 
file "Xorg.0.log-GMA3650" is obvious, also I don't understand way it happened.

Does someone have a hint how to change the configuration, so that the nice 
fanless boxes will work in higher display resolutions too, up to 1920x1080?

Tanks, Stephan
