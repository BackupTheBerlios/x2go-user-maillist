<snip>
If you are having problems running X over SSH, you probably won't be
able to run X2Go - at least that's my guess.  Is
your /etc/ssh/sshd_config set up to allow X? You may wish to try the
following settings:

X11Forwarding yes
X11DisplayOffset 10
X11UseLocalhost no

Good luck - John
